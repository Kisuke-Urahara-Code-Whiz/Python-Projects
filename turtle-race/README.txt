Rules:

1) There are 6 colours to choose : red, orange, yellow, blue, purple, green
2) Write a valid colour name in the pop up dialog box.
3) Placing bet on a colour not present there lead to termination of the race.
4) Winning and losing message of the race is printed on the console as well as the screeen.
5) After a message pops up on the screen,click on the screen to terminate the game.
